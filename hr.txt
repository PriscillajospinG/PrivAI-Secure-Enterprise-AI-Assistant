1. Introduction

This document outlines the human resources policies of ABC Technologies Pvt Ltd. These policies are designed to ensure a productive, respectful, and professional working environment for all employees.

2. Working Hours

Standard working hours are from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.

Employees are expected to:
- Log into the company system by 9:00 AM.
- Maintain at least 8 hours of productive work daily.
- Inform their reporting manager in case of late arrival.

Flexible working hours may be granted with manager approval.

3. Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to the following leaves per year:

- Casual Leave: 12 days
- Sick Leave: 10 days
- Earned Leave: 15 days
- Public Holidays: As per company calendar

Leave requests must be submitted through the internal HR portal at least 2 days in advance.

Emergency leave must be communicated to the manager immediately.

4. Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with prior approval.

During remote work, employees must:
- Be available on company communication tools
- Attend scheduled meetings
- Maintain productivity standards

5. Code of Conduct

All employees must maintain professional conduct at the workplace.

The following behaviors are strictly prohibited:
- Harassment or discrimination
- Misuse of company resources
- Sharing confidential company data externally

Violations may result in disciplinary action.

6. IT Security Policy

Employees must follow IT security best practices:

- Do not share passwords.
- Use company-approved software only.
- Report suspicious emails or cybersecurity threats to the IT team immediately.

All company systems are monitored for security purposes.

7. Expense Reimbursement

Employees may claim reimbursements for approved business expenses including:

- Travel
- Accommodation
- Client meetings

Expense claims must be submitted within 7 days using the finance portal.
