PrivAI Enterprise Leave Policy

1. Introduction
This policy outlines the leave entitlements for all PrivAI employees.

2. Annual Leave
Employees are entitled to 25 days of paid annual leave per calendar year. Leave requests must be submitted at least two weeks in advance.

3. Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave. A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

4. Parental Leave
PrivAI provides 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave for primary caregivers and 4 weeks for secondary caregivers.

5. Remote Work
Employees can work remotely up to 3 days per week, subject to team requirements and manager approval.
